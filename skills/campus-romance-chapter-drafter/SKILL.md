# Campus Romance Chapter Drafter

Use this skill when writing `chapters/chapter_xx.md`.

## Required Context

Read only the necessary files:

- `planning/story_bible.md`
- `chapters/chapter_xx_plan.md`
- previous chapter ending if this is not chapter 1
- relevant entries from `reviews/continuity_log.md`

## Chapter Requirements

- Target length: 2,700-3,300 Chinese characters unless instructed otherwise.
- Each chapter must contain one concrete event.
- Open close to disturbance, embarrassment, pressure, desire, or unanswered question.
- Use scenes and dialogue; avoid long explanation.
- End with a changed relationship state or an unresolved question.
- Keep the romance suitable for high-school minors: restrained, non-explicit, emotionally focused.

## Campus Romance Engines

Choose 2-3 per chapter:

- office encounter
- mistaken notebook, worksheet, or message
- public ranking or teacher comment
- classmate teasing or false rumor
- task partnership forced by English teacher
- late self-study or rainy commute
- QQ space, SMS, or old phone misunderstanding
- competition, speech, sports meet, class duty
- family pressure crossing into school life
- object proof such as red pen mark, bus ticket, folded worksheet, umbrella, vocabulary list

## Drafting Style

- Prefer concrete 温州 and ten-years-ago details: bus stop, paper tests, QQ, school uniform, typhoon-season rain, classroom fans, evening self-study, English listening broadcast.
- Let the characters hide more than they say.
- Make dialogue short and slightly evasive.
- Avoid slogan-like summaries after emotional moments.
- Do not overuse metaphors or decorative punctuation.

## Anti-AI Gate

Before finalizing, scan for and rewrite narration-level patterns:

- `不是X，而是Y`
- `问题不在于X`
- `关键在于`
- `值得注意的是`
- `这不仅是...更是...`
- `这就是...的意义`
- summary endings that explain the theme

Replace them with action, image, consequence, or a line of dialogue.

## Output

Write only the chapter title and正文 in the chapter file. Do not include craft notes inside the prose file.
