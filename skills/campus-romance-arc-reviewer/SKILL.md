# Campus Romance Arc Reviewer

Use this after every 5 chapters and before entering a new arc.

## Inputs

- `planning/story_bible.md`
- `planning/outline.md`
- `reviews/continuity_log.md`
- chapters completed in the current arc

## Review Questions

1. Does every chapter have a memorable small event?
2. Is the relationship changing, or only circling the same mood?
3. Are misunderstandings varied and believable?
4. Are classmates, teachers, and family pressure creating public stakes?
5. Is the romance still restrained and age-appropriate?
6. Are 温州 and ten-years-ago details recurring naturally?
7. Are chapter endings strong enough for serial reading?
8. Are there repeated objects, locations, or beats that need variation?
9. Which伏笔 must be paid off in the next 5 chapters?
10. Does the story still point toward the open university-separation ending?

## Repair Actions

- If too flat: add a public arena such as ranking, speech, class duty, teacher conversation, or rumor.
- If too sweet: add cost, timing, or outside witness.
- If too painful: add one concrete act of care.
- If too repetitive: move the next event outside the office.
- If too vague: bind emotion to an object or school task.

## Output

Append to `reviews/arc_reviews.md`:

- chapters reviewed
- strengths
- risks
- required repairs
- next arc priorities
