# Campus Romance Continuity Editor

Use this after drafting or revising a chapter.

## Inputs

- `planning/story_bible.md`
- `planning/outline.md`
- `reviews/continuity_log.md`
- the chapter plan
- the chapter draft
- previous chapter when needed

## Checks

### Canon

- Names, classes, roles, teachers, family backgrounds.
- School year, exam cycle, weather/season, and daily schedule.
- 温州 and ten-years-ago details do not feel anachronistic.

### Relationship State

Track the relationship as one of:

1. 陌生但注意到
2. 熟悉但装作普通
3. 互相试探
4. 暗中偏心
5. 流言压力
6. 刻意疏远
7. 承认喜欢但不承诺
8. 毕业后开放分别

The chapter may move one step, rarely two. Do not skip emotional cause.

### Event Logic

- The chapter has a visible event.
- The event has a cause and consequence.
- Any misunderstanding has a believable reason, not forced silence only.
- Teachers and classmates act like people, not plot devices.

###伏笔

Record:

- new objects or lines that can return later
- unresolved misunderstandings
- exam/ranking changes
- family pressure introduced
- promised tasks or appointments

## Output

Update `reviews/continuity_log.md` with:

- chapter number
- time/date or school calendar moment
- event summary
- relationship state change
- new伏笔
- continuity risks

If a draft fails, produce a short repair list before the next revision.
