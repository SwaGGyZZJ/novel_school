# Campus Romance Style Polisher

Use this after a chapter draft is structurally acceptable.

## Do Not Change

- Canon facts
- Chapter event order
- Relationship state
- Ending hook
- Scale boundary for minors

## Polish Goals

1. Make prose more human and less template-like.
2. Replace explanation with detail.
3. Make characters speak like high-school students.
4. Strengthen 温州/十年前 texture without dumping facts.
5. Preserve酸涩 and restraint.

## Rewrite Moves

- Convert emotional labels into gestures:
  - `她很难过` -> hand stops over the answer sheet, one blank stays unfilled.
- Convert theme explanation into object image:
  - a folded worksheet, wrong notebook, umbrella, bus ticket, red pen mark.
- Convert direct confession into deflection:
  - `你是不是喜欢我` can become `你最近是不是老往我们班门口走？`
- Convert generic beauty into situated detail:
  - humid stairwell, chalk dust, English office kettle, evening study bell.

## Remove

- Formulaic contrasts: `不是...而是...`
- Teaching-tone transitions: `关键在于`, `值得注意的是`
- Summary morals after scenes
- Excessive `——`
- Over-neat emotional analysis
- Adult-sounding romantic lines

## Keep

- Specific mistakes
- Awkward silences
- Classmate interruptions
- Teacher routines
- Exam pressure
- The sense that they are good students doing one small forbidden thing badly

## Output

Return or save the polished chapter prose. If reporting changes, summarize only the main style repairs.
